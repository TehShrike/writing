--- title: "Books to Read for Designers" layout: post ---

There is no magical list of books to read that will make you a great designer. And there there is a vast amount of knowledge out there to learn from. The following is a list of books that I have found to be quite useful in making me a better designer. They are the books that I read over and over again.

  • On Writing Well - William Zinser
  • Grid Systems
  • The Form of the Book - Jan Tschichold
  • Elements of Typographic Style
  • Interaction of Color - Josef Albers
